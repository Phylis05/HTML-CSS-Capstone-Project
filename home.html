HIGH SCHOOL SCIENCE CONTEST 2020

An opportunity created with the intention to grow the science knowledge and skills of high school students across the country is taking place in July 2020, in Nairobi.

2020.07.16(Thur)~ 17 (Fri)
@KICC Art Centre and main hall

Main Program

Lecture

Listen to the speakers from various counties and countries about the messages of learning and sharing knowledge

Exhibition

Presentation of science projects by the competing teams as judges and other paricipants rates the projects.

Forum

There will be an opportunity for the students to interact with the great speakers and other high school teachers.

Networking

Attendants will get an opportunity to network and know other people in the science department nationwide.
