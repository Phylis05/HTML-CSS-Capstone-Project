"Hello Learning World!!"

HOME SCHOOLERS MEETUP EVENT 2020

An social opportunity created for all home schoolers and their guardiants with the intention to enable exchange of knowledge among participants across Kenya in July 2020, in Nairobi.

2020.07.16(Thur)~ 17 (Fri)
@Uhuru Gargens, Art Centre and Main all

Main Program

Lecture

Listen to the speakers from various counties and countries about the messages of learning and sharing knowledge

Discussions

The learners will have a hands-on session where they will be given certail topics to discuss in groups.

Forum

There will be an opportunity for the students to interact with the great speakers and other high school teachers.

Networking

Attendants will get an opportunity to network and know other people who practice the same kind of schooling as they.
